Czinger 21C
2023 - date		  

Profile

LA based manufacturer Czinger brings state of the art 3D printing technology to the hypercar scene with the spectacular 21C.

A hybrid powertrain couples a twin-turbo V8 (revving at 11000rpm) with twin electric motors to produce a peak 1250hp, pushed through an Xtrac 7-speed sequential transmission. With less than 1250kg to move, the 21C hits a claimed 281mph, with 60mph arrining in under 2 seconds along the way. Although still a work in progress, the 21C is also now the proud holder of the the Laguna Seca road car lap record with a remarkable 1min 25sec (2 seconds quicker than a Senna).

Design of the 21C was apparently inspired by the SR-71 Blackbird jet fighter, with massive wing-like dihedral doors. The aeroanutic theme continues inside with the tandem 1+1 seating.
