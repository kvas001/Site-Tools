Koenigsegg Jesko
2022 - date		  

Profile

TRevealed at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show, the awesome Koenigsegg Jesko takes over from the Agera RS as the Swede's track focused 'megacar'.

Named after the father of Koenigsegg founder Christian, the Jesko is powered by a lightweight 5 litre twin-turbo V8, producing up to 1600bhp on E85 Biofuel. With just 1300kg to move, the predicted 0-300kmh time of 12 sec is brutal.

This megacar is available in two guises. The first is the track enhanced version, with high-tech aero offering up to 1000kg of downforce. The second version (the 'Absolut') is designed more for top speed, with Koenigsegg prediciting a staggering 330mph max!!!

The Jesko is littered with cutting edge technology such as active rear wheel steering, a revolving dashboard display, and their unique Light Speed Transmission (LST). This 9-speed gearbox can features no less than 7 clutches for instant shifts between any gears.
