9FF GT9 Vmax
2012 - date		  

Profile

The 1400ps 9FF GT9 Vmax has a verified 272mph top speed, making it the world's fastest road legal car (Koenigsegg claim 273mph for the One:1, but this awaits ratification).

Porsche's 997 provides the starting point for the Vmax. The engine is then replaced by the unit from a GT3 RSR racecar, mid mounted and enlarged to 4.2 litres, then twin turbocharged to a phenomenal 1381bhp and 856 lbft of torque. A widened lightweight carbon fibre body and integral roll cage are fitted, but 9FF will trim the interior to whatever suits your taste.

Less than half a million pounds for the world's fastest car is an absolute bargain. It seems incredulous that this race car in all but name is road legal, but thankfully that is the case!
